About

I'm a Computer Science undergraduate who learned data the unglamorous way first — reconciling vendor contracts, tracing hardware failures to their root cause, syncing sensor data that kept falling out of order — before turning that same instinct toward applied AI: language models, topic clustering, and agent pipelines that actually ship.

That combination shows up in how I build. Procurement audits and IoT sensor pipelines taught me that; NLP and LLM projects are where I get to apply it.

Right now I'm exploring multimodal AI and rapid prototyping — moving fast from a research idea to a working, evaluable system.
